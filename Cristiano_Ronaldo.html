About Cristiano Ronaldo's Story

Ronaldo was born in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal on February 5, 1985. His father, Jos√© Dinis Aveiro, was the equipment manager for the local football club, Andorinha. Ronaldo's name was chosen to honor his father's favorite movie actor, Ronald Reagan. Ronaldo played for Clube Desportivo Nacional de Madeira before moving to Sporting Clube de Portugal, where he played for their youth teams. He made his debut for Sporting's first team in 2002. At age 16, Ronaldo signed with Manchester United in a record deal for a player of his age. He won the FA Cup in his first season and went on to win three consecutive Premier League titles.Ronaldo has won the Ballon d'Or five times and led Portugal to victory in the 2016 European Championship. He is also the first player to score 900 career goals.
